Ashley's Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Prep Time: 5-10 mins
Cook Time: 5 mins
Servings: 4-5

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries
  • 1 fresh banana, peeled
  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup ferbreeze cocktail
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Toppings

  • 1 fresh strawberry
  • Whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Dice the strawberries.
  2. Slice the banana into round wedges.
  3. Add the ice, strawberries, banana, vanilla, milk, honey and cocktail into a blender.
  4. Blend for 2-4 minutes non-stop.
  5. Pour and serve with whipped cream and strawberry.

Protip: Serve in summer for extra smoothie-ness!
